Why B (Studio)?

  • Constants/units table always visible — solves discoverability directly
  • Searchable sidebar makes finding constants fast
  • Single-page layout is easy to understand
  • Natural room for equation templates (tabs in sidebar)
  • Still focused enough for quick calculations
