Design — Section 4 (Revised): Equations Format

Updated: multiple named expressions per equation file, each with its own "Add" button.

Updated Markdown File Format

--- title: Escape Velocity category: Mechanics tags: [velocity, gravity, compact objects] params: - symbol: M default: "1.4 M_sun" description: Mass of the object - symbol: R default: "10 km" description: Radius expressions: - name: "Escape velocity" expression: "sqrt(2 G M / R) in km/s" - name: "Circular velocity" expression: "sqrt(G M / R) in km/s" description: "Circular orbital velocity, 1/√2 times escape velocity" --- The escape velocity from a body of mass $M$ and radius $R$ is: $$v_{\rm esc} = \sqrt{\frac{2GM}{R}}$$
